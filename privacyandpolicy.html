Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect
  • Personal Information: Name, contact number, email ID, date of birth, gender, address, resume/CV, educational and professional background.
  • Employer Information: Company name, contact person, contact details, job requirements, and hiring preferences.
  • Website Usage Data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent, and other analytics collected through cookies or third-party tools.
2. Purpose of Data Collection
  • Providing recruitment and placement services
  • Matching candidates with suitable job opportunities
  • Communicating with candidates and clients about job openings, interviews, and updates
  • Improving our website and services
  • Fulfilling legal or regulatory obligations
3. Confidentiality and Data Sharing
  • All personal data is treated with strict confidentiality.
  • We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties.
  • Data may be shared only with:
    • Potential employers or clients relevant to your job profile
    • Trusted partners assisting in our service delivery
    • Government or legal authorities, if mandated by law
4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to secure your data against unauthorized access, misuse, alteration, or loss.

5. Your Rights
  • Access, update, or correct your personal data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent where applicable

You can exercise these rights by reaching out to us through the appropriate channels.

6. Cookies Policy

Our website uses cookies to improve user experience and gather analytics. You may disable cookies through your browser settings at any time.

7. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of those websites. We advise you to review their privacy policies separately.

8. Changes to this Policy

We may revise this Privacy Policy without prior notice. Any updates will be posted on this page along with the effective date of the changes.
