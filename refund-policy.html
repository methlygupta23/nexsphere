Refund Policy

Our services are designed to deliver value digitally/remotely once they are availed. Services once availed will not be refundable.

However, we understand that circumstances may change. If you wish to cancel your service booking, a refund will only be applicable if cancellation is made within 24 hours of booking.

Beyond this period, no refunds will be processed as resources and commitments are already allocated for your service.

For any refund-related queries, please reach out to us via our contact page.
