Frequently Asked Questions

1. What does Nexsphere Global do?

Nexsphere Global is a recruitment and HR solutions company that helps businesses hire the right candidates for non-IT roles. We specialize in hiring for sales, marketing, HR, finance, telecalling, operations, and more.

2. What kind of roles do you hire for?

We focus on non-technical roles, including:
  • Sales Executives & Managers
  • Telecallers / BPO Staff
  • HR Executives & Recruiters
  • Digital Marketing & Social Media Roles
  • Business Development
  • Customer Support
  • Finance & Accounts
  • Field Staff & Coordinators

3. What is your hiring process?

Our standard process includes:
  1. Understanding the job requirement
  2. Sourcing and screening suitable candidates
  3. Sharing shortlisted profiles
  4. Coordinating interviews
  5. Supporting final selection & follow-up

4. Do you charge for your services?

Yes, we charge a ₹999 token of engagement to begin the process. The final service fee depends on the role and is shared in advance. We follow transparent and flexible pricing.

5. How quickly will we get candidates?

We usually share profiles within 48 to 72 hours after the requirement is confirmed. However, timelines may vary based on the role and location.

6. What happens if a candidate doesn’t join?

We provide a free replacement if the selected candidate doesn’t join or leaves within the agreed period. Our goal is to ensure you get a reliable hire.

7. How can we get started with Nexsphere Global?

You can contact us via WhatsApp, phone, or email to share your requirement. Once the ₹999 token is paid, we start working on your hiring request immediately.

8. Do you verify the candidates?

Yes, we pre-screen all candidates through calls and checks before sharing their profiles. We ensure they meet the basic qualifications and job criteria.

9. Do you hire for freshers or internships?

Yes, we provide candidates for fresher roles and internships as well, especially for sales, marketing, and admin positions.

10. Which locations do you serve?

We offer PAN India recruitment services. Whether your company is based in a metro city or a smaller town, we can help with local hiring.
